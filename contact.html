FAQ

Questions, answered.

A few of the questions we hear most often. If yours isn't here, write to us — we read every message.

How long does a typical project take?
A kitchen refresh can take 3–6 weeks; a full villa build is typically 6–12 months from groundbreaking. We'll give you a realistic timeline at the design stage and stick to it.
Do you handle both design and construction?
Yes. We're a design-build studio — one contract, one accountable team, from first sketch to final handover. The same hands that design it also sign off every joint.
What's the cost of an initial consultation?
The first consultation is always free. We use it to understand your brief, walk through your site (or look at drawings), and work out whether we're a good fit.
Do you work outside Kampala?
Yes — we've delivered projects across Uganda and East Africa. Travel and accommodation for site visits get itemised separately so it's transparent.
Can you work with my budget?
We take projects from modest renovations to premium builds. After the first meeting we'll tell you honestly what's possible within your range — and if it isn't, we'll say so.
