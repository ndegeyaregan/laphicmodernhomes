Our Disciplines

Six disciplines.
One disciplined studio.

From the first hand-drawn line to the last shrub planted, LAPHIC works in five quiet conversations — construction, architecture, interior, exterior, and landscape — backed by a sixth, our 3D studio, that lets you walk through a project long before it is built.

01 — Architectural Design

Drawing places that belong to their site.

Every project starts on paper, with a slow conversation about light, climate, family life, and the long view from the road. We work site-first — sketching, modelling, and refining until the building feels inevitable.

  • Site analysis and feasibility studies
  • Concept, schematic and detailed design
  • Authority submissions and approvals
  • Construction documentation
Architectural Services
02 — Interior Design

Interiors that age with grace.

Gypsum, painting, custom furniture, aquariums, curtains and lighting — we coordinate every interior trade so the finish feels considered, layered, and quietly luxurious from day one.

  • Concept boards and material palettes
  • Bespoke joinery and furniture
  • Lighting design and finishes
  • Full project execution and styling
Interior Services
03 — Exterior & Facade

Facades that hold the weather.

From cladding and finishes to porches, paving and outdoor lighting — exterior work that protects what we build inside, and gives the street something to look at for the next forty years.

  • Facade design and weatherproofing
  • Premium exterior finishes
  • Paving, decks and outdoor structures
  • Roofing and rainwater detailing
Exterior Services
04 — Landscape Architecture

Gardens that improve with time.

We design landscapes that knit a building into its ground — patient planting, durable hardscape, and water that moves the way it should. Less ornament. More years.

  • Garden masterplans and planting design
  • Hardscape, paths and water features
  • Irrigation and drainage
  • Long-term maintenance plans
Landscape Services
05 — Construction & Build

One accountable hand from foundation to handover.

Our build arm carries the drawings into the field — the same team that designed your home will sign off every pour, every joint, every snag. One contract, one accountability.

  • Main contracting and project management
  • Quality and safety on every site
  • Trusted, vetted subcontractor network
  • Transparent cost reporting
Construction Services
06 — 3D Visualisation

Walk the home before we break ground.

Photorealistic interior and exterior renders, walk-throughs and short films — so you can stand inside your future living room, judge the light, and change your mind while it is still cheap to do so.

  • Photorealistic stills and animations
  • VR walk-throughs of key spaces
  • Material and lighting studies
  • Marketing visuals for developers
3D Experience
07 — Aluminium Works

Gates, gratings & cladding that hold the long view.

Custom aluminium gates, balustrades, screens and cladding — fabricated in-house, finished to last. From precision louvres to feature entrance gates, we draw, weld, and install every line ourselves.

  • Bespoke aluminium gates and entrances
  • Window frames, louvres and shutters
  • Balustrades, screens and stair rails
  • Facade cladding and feature panels
Aluminium Services
